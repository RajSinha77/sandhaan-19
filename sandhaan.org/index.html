About

The techno-management fest SANDHAAN is an august concoction of startling events which concots an adrenaline rush among the masses. Ever since its inception SANDHAAN has endeavoured to live and promote innovation and technical prowess. The pulse of BIT beats harder as it approaches near. The much anticipated fest with participants from all over the country find their nook and muster lifetime experiences. Model Club being the protagonist of the play, has augmented year by year in delivering this event of gargantuan magnitude.Giving subjection to school and college students from near and far , it gives a testimony of a crowd of approximately 8000 peeple
